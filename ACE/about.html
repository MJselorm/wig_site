The Maison Crème Story

Crafted by Hand,

Every piece that leaves our atelier carries over 40 hours of meticulous craftsmanship — a testament to our unwavering pursuit of perfection.

Four Pillars of Excellence

Our Artisanal Process

01

Ethical Sourcing

Our journey begins at the source. We partner exclusively with ethical hair collectors across Southeast Asia and South America, ensuring every strand is sourced with dignity and fair compensation. Only the finest virgin hair — unprocessed, cuticle-aligned, and single-donor — earns the Maison Crème designation.

02

Hand-Tied Construction

Each unit is constructed by our master artisans using the single-knot hand-tying technique. This painstaking method — where individual strands are tied one-by-one onto Swiss lace — creates the most natural movement and an undetectable hairline. A single unit requires over 30 hours of tying alone.

03

Quality Inspection

Before any piece is deemed ready, it passes through our rigorous 12-point quality inspection. From knot density to lace transparency, hair texture consistency to cap comfort — every detail is scrutinized. Units that don't meet our exacting standards are returned to the atelier for refinement.

04

The Finishing Touch

The final stage is where art meets science. Each unit is custom-styled, pre-plucked along the hairline, and finished with hand-placed baby hairs. The result is a piece that doesn't just sit on your head — it becomes an extension of you. Ready to wear, ready to transform.

What We Stand For

Our Commitments

◈

Sustainability

We use biodegradable packaging, carbon-neutral shipping, and maintain zero-waste practices in our atelier. Luxury should never cost the earth.

✦

Fair Trade

Every hair collector in our supply chain is compensated at 3x the market rate. We believe that ethical sourcing is non-negotiable.

▣

Longevity

Our units are built to last 18-24 months with proper care. We offer complimentary maintenance guides and a lifetime repair service.
